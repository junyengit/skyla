The Skyla Experience

LA Like You've Never Seen It

Perched atop 6100 Wilshire, Skyla offers an unmatched vantage point over the iconic Los Angeles skyline — from the Hollywood Hills to the Downtown towers.

The Observation Lounge — Floor-to-ceiling glass, 360° views
◎

360° Observation Deck

Step outside onto our open-air deck and take in sweeping views of Downtown LA, the Santa Monica Mountains, Century City, and on clear days — all the way to the San Gabriel Mountains.

☕

Rooftop Café & Lounge

Sip handcrafted matcha lattes and single-origin pour-overs while watching the LA sun dip behind the hills. Our indoor lounge is an intimate escape above the city buzz.

◐

Golden Hour & Night Views

Book a sunset or evening visit and watch the city transform into a sea of sparkling lights stretching from the hills to Downtown.

◈

Perfect for Photos

Capture the quintessential LA shot. Wide-open sky, zero obstruction, and a horizon that stretches endlessly. Your followers will never forgive you for missing this.
